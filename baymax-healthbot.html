Medications

Amoxicillin 500mg

Purpose: Antibiotic for bacterial infection

Ibuprofen 400mg

Take 1 tablet as needed for pain, not more than 3 times a day

Purpose: Pain relief and fever reduction

Important Instructions

  • Complete the full course of Amoxicillin even if you feel better
  • Take Amoxicillin with food to reduce stomach upset
  • Do not take Ibuprofen on an empty stomach

Contact emergency services or visit an emergency room if you experience:

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • Confusion or inability to wake up
  • Bluish lips or face
